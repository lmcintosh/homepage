Recommendations for Mexican Food in San Diego, CA

Background

It seems that running a quick google search for the best Mexican food in San Diego yields only sit-down places that are perhaps a little less authentic.

My recommendations here are all hole-in-the-wall places (with the exception of one) that have absolutely fantastic food.

Rico's Taco Shop

Go here for the sauce and the California burritos.

165 South El Camino Real

Encinitas, CA 92024-4140

(760) 944-7689

Jorge's Mexicatessen

Go here for the chicken soup with huge chunks of fresh avocado. The chicken and rice burrito is also a favorite.

267 North El Camino Real

Encinitas, CA 92024-5366

(760) 942-6809

Filiberto's

The only 24-hour hole-in-the-wall place I can think of. Be warned, some shadiness sometimes goes on here at night. Go for the carnitas fries.

476 South Coast Highway 101

Encinitas, CA 92024-3529

(760) 436-8001

Mexico Viejo Restaurant

I've heard of people driving all the way from L.A. to eat one of their three-taco burritos.

1465 Encinitas Boulevard

Encinitas, CA 92024-2951

(760) 944-9556

Roberto's Mexican Food

Similar to Filiberto's, but in Solana Beach.

445 North Highway 101

Solana Beach, CA 92075-1131

(858) 259-0042

Fidel's Little Mexico

The exception to the list - while not a hole-in-the-wall, this is nonetheless a fun place to eat and has decent food. Pricier than the others.

749 Genevieve Street

Solana Beach, CA 92075-2035

(858) 755-5292

Saxony Market

This is another exception to the list; but this place is awesome. It's a market on the side of the road along one of my favorite roads in San Diego (Saxony Rd.), and sells some pretty awesome salsa and guacamole, in addition to a selection of fresh fruits and produce locally grown.

La Costa Avenue and Saxony Rd

Encinitas, CA 92024

(760) 753-4285
